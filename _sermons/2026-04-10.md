None of us are mind readers.  That means that if there are things that you
think should be obvious to your faculty mentors or to me as your DGS and you
have not brought it up, it is not safe to assume that it’s actually obvious to
us!  Please use the SPR as a time to have a conversation with your mentors
about things you think are going well or poorly, things you’re worried about,
and places where you are unsure about expectations.  And if you have concerns
that I can help with, please just tell me about them.  I think most of the
faculty are trying to do their best by students, and a lot of us feel bad when
things go sideways for reasons that we “should have seen coming.”  But… again,
none of us are mind readers.  So talk to us!
