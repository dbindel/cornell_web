Monday is the MLK day holiday.  The Cornell library has a collection of
resources related to Martin Luther King, including a list of his books here:
 
<https://guides.library.cornell.edu/mlk/books>
 
If you want to read a sermon this week, these are far better sermons in these
books than anything I might give you.   And it’s a useful reminder of where
this country has been, how it has changed, and how we still need to work to
change it.
