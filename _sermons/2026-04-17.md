We are down to the last 2.5 weeks of instruction!  I wish you good luck to all
of you with the end of the semester, whether that means finishing classes,
writing (and then grading) final exams, A and B exams, or making it to that
next paper deadline.  And in a time when short-term deadlines tend to pile up
in front of us, I wish for you the perspective to see past the pile and
remember your longer term goals and aspirations.  That next paper deadline is
part of you telling your story to the world; that A or B exam is about you
staking out the path you think your dissertation research might take (or
reviewing the path that it took). And that final you are taking or giving is an
assessment, but it is also a motivation to review and synthesize in a way that
crystallizes the knowledge from a course into something that (one hopes) lives
in the mind of the students beyond the end of the semester and well into the
future.
